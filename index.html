Who is this Elm anyway?

Elm is functional programming language that compiles to JavaScript.

  • 🚀fast

  • 💎hard to break

  • 🔬easily testable

  • 📐profoundly maintainable

Why Should I care about Elm?

Many of the common issues that front-end developers have to deal with just don’t exist in Elm.
